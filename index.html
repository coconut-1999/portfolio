About me

Hi, I am Paula – Würzburg (Germany) native, tea enthusiast, and freshly graduated communication designer! I'm passionate about visual concepts that not only look good but also make sense.

During my studies, I developed a knack for clean design, strategic thinking, and storytelling, always with the goal of making complex ideas easy to understand, engaging, and tailored to the target audience.

My strengths lie in digital product (UI/UX) design, graphic design, and illustration. I'm excited to keep growing as a designer, learn new things, and help bring great ideas to life, ideally as part of a team that loves creating just as much as I do.
