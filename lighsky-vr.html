LightSky VR

LightSky VR

Interaction Design
Virtual Reality

Those of us who live in cities rarely, if ever, see the night sky in its purest form. The reason for this is light pollution.

LightSky VR offers a highly immersive experience that allows you to travel to different locations with varying levels of light pollution and compare their night skies.

Detailed information is also available for each location, and you can discover fascinating light pollution facts as you travel between them.

Year: 2024 ┃6th semester

Tools:

A-Frame
Blender
Photoshop
Figma
